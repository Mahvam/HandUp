HOW IT WORKS

Simple on both sides.
Powerful in the middle.

Whether you need a lift or want to give one, HandUp gets you there in minutes — with dignity, privacy, and a human connection at the center.

01
Tell the AI what's going on
Our AI asks you three warm questions and writes your story with dignity. No forms. No shame. Just a conversation that turns your situation into a need card.
02
Choose how you appear
Use your first name, a nickname, or a description. Your identity is verified privately by HandUp. Donors see our shield — not your personal details.
03
One person steps up
A donor sees your full need, taps "Lift them up," and covers it entirely. You're notified immediately. Funds arrive within 24 hours.
04
Say thank you your way
A voice memo, a short video, or a few words. Your message goes directly — and only — to the person who lifted you up. That's the moment that changes everything.
WHY HANDUP IS DIFFERENT

Built for dignity.
Not for campaigns.

GoFundMe asks dozens of strangers to chip in. HandUp asks one person to step up entirely. That's not a small difference — it's a completely different human experience.

AI story coach
Can't find the words? Our AI interviews you and writes your story — with heart, clarity, and dignity. Three questions. One compelling need card.
Alias protection
Use any name you want publicly. HandUp verifies your real identity privately and securely. Donors see our shield — not your details.
Voice, video, or written
Recipients choose how they say thank you — voice memo, short video, or a written note. No pressure. Any response is deeply personal.
Funds in 24 hours
Not a progress bar. Not 2 to 5 days. One person claims the full need and money moves fast. Because when someone needs help, they need it now.
TRUST AND SAFETY

Every person is real.
Every need is verified.

HandUp's verification keeps donors safe and recipients protected — without ever exposing what people don't want shared.

Government ID verification
Every recipient confirms their real identity privately with HandUp before their card goes live. Stored securely. Never shown publicly.
Need confirmation
Recipients share a photo or document showing their specific need. Prescription labels, receipts, bank statements — blurred details, confirmed truth.
100% to the recipient
Every dollar you give goes directly to the person you chose. HandUp is kept running by optional donor tips — never by touching the recipient's funds.
Founder-reviewed at launch
Every early recipient is personally verified by HandUp's founder. A real human, reviewing real people, with real needs. That's our launch promise.
